My face is flushed red from embarrassment even. Believe me.


All jokes aside, I suppose the reason is that simple blogs like these really can have an unique charm I've always liked.

One time, I went on a really old (maybe 15 years old?) Japanese website, and it had a very long diary spanning years of a guy's journey and experiences with his Lasik surgery. I had been thinking of getting Lasik (although, I opted for contacts now), and it was a very interesting read despite how old it was.
He updated it for years and years. Not only after a couple days, weeks or months. Which is really what made it stand out.
